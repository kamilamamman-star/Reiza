Drilling Engineering Consultancy

Geothermal Energy Advisory

Geothermal energy is West Africa's most underexplored clean energy resource — and REIZA is one of the few Nigerian consultancies actively building expertise in this space. We provide technical advisory for geothermal project development, including feasibility assessment and integration with existing oil & gas infrastructure.

Our approach is grounded in the recognition that the skills and engineering principles of petroleum drilling are highly transferable to geothermal — making Nigeria's existing technical workforce a key asset in geothermal development.

Geothermal feasibility assessments
Resource potential evaluation
Petroleum-to-geothermal repurposing
Technical advisory & project support
Regulatory landscape analysis
Investment case preparation
